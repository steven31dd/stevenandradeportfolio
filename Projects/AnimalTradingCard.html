Chimpanzee

Human and chimp DNA is so similar because the two species are so closely related.

  • Habitat: The dense jungle forest of Africa
  • Food: fruits, leaves, insects, bark, eggs and small animals
  • Strength: They are five to eight times stronger than humans
  • Social: they tend to stay in groups, consisting of up to about 50 individuals and comprising of several family groups.

Chimpanzees have black hair, not fur, sometimes have white whiskers on their chin and they have bare skin on their palms, hands, and soles of their feet. Their opposable thumbs and big toes enable a precision grip on branches.
